ABOUT

Hyderabad, the capital of Telangana, is a vibrant city with a rich history and culture. It's known for its historical landmarks like the Charminar and Golkonda Fort, its delicious Hyderabadi cuisine, and its modern IT industry. The city is a blend of traditional and modern, with a unique cultural heritage shaped by centuries of Muslim rule and a more recent influx of technology companies.

Mahatma Gandhi

Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi was an Indian lawyer, anti-colonial nationalist, and political ethicist who employed nonviolent resistance to lead the successful campaign for India's independence from British rule.

Jawaharlal Nehru

Jawaharlal Nehru was an Indian anti-colonial nationalist, secular humanist, social democrat, and statesman who was a central figure in India during the middle of the 20th century.

B.R. Ambedkar

Bhimrao Ramji Ambedkar was an Indian jurist, economist, social reformer and statesman who chaired the committee that drafted the Constitution of India based on the debates of the Constituent Assembly of India.

Sarvepalli RadhaKrishnan

Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan BR was an Indian academician, philosopher and statesman who served as the President of India from 1962 to 1967.
